Marvell Nanolab

I work as one of the process student staff in the nanolab in Sutardja Dai Hall. From a purely job description-based perspective, our task is to run a monthly quality monitor of all the equipment in the lab, which entails running the equipment on silicon and aluminum wafers on a regular basis to make sure nothing is amiss.

Joining the nanolab staff was my first real experience working in a lab, and a better environment could not have been asked for. Not only are the rest of the staff friendly, wicked good at what they do, and always willing to help the newbie out to make sure things don't explode (literally), the researchers and other student staff are also great to chat with when you're both waiting on a process to run.
