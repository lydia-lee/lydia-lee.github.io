A Brief Summary

I was born and raised in Texas and have lived there all my life. Currently, I am a third year undergraduate at the University of California, Berkeley.

Although my major is in electrical engineering and computer science, my interests extend beyond those two fields and into subjects pertaining to biology and chemistry. From my time as one of the staff in the Marvell Nanolab, I've developed an appreciation for materials science and welcome any discussion of interesting materials (this one is my personal favorite) or fabrication techniques.

More recently, my interest in chemistry, combined with the belief that cost should never be a barrier to advancement, has driven me to explore maker culture's intersection with academia and pedagogy as part of a recent project at UC Berkeley.
