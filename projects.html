Team Turret: Meet the Engineers

This undertaking actually began as part of our final project for our intro to microelectronic circuits class, but it quickly turned into us having way too much fun with the thought of a working, controllable turret.

I had the opportunity to work with two amazing partners, Andy Wong (B.S. Mechanical Engineering '17) and Glen Chou (B.S. Mechanical Engineering and EECS '17):
Andy
Glen

The project specs required that we introduce one extra analog element to what we'd done already for our lab robot. For our turret, we introduced several additional analog components in addition to some digital circuitry (schematics can be found here).

Many thanks to PiE for their incredibly helpful advice, witty late-night conversations, and for letting us work all day (and all night) in 101!
