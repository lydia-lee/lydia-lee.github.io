Blender

Blender is an open-source 3D graphics and animation software with capabilities comparable to those of Autodesk Maya and a UI comparable to that of the USS Vincennes. Thankfully, we have Google Translate (or in this case, just Google), so there are ways for getting around the learning curve in Blender! Blender has its own series of tutorials that you can find on their page, but there are also some written tutorials of varying quality and maintenance on their wiki.

I was introduced to Blender by a friend at the start of my sophomore year of university but never quite had the time to play with it much. What little I've managed to tinker with can be found here and here.
